Doggos

Doggos is a social media website developed by a team of students that helps pet owners connect with other owners in order to establish a friendly relationship with one another. Here you can explore, match, and block other users similar to that of a typical social media site. You can also customize your profile picture and edit your information as you wish.

Made with:
- Javacript, ExpressJS, PostgresSQL, HTML5, CSS3

Bullet RPG

Bullet RPG is a game in developed by a group of friends where you get to play as a character and shoot various types of enemies in order to obtain coins. There is a shop where you can spend your coins in order to buy weapons and consumables to help aide your character for upcoming battles. The game is still in development and is continued to be worked on.

Made with:
- Javacript, Phaser, HTML5, CSS3
